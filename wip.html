WORK IN PROGRESS

I've been making a wall hanging that fits over our new piano. Besides making something that felt interesting in the space, I also wanted to use up some of the bits and bobs from my studio, and make something that I could do a little more organic vs. highly structured.

I started by winding a warp made of various materials and colors. I blended them together organically so I got light sections and dark sections. I also got a little gold in there for fun and sparkle!

I decided on a twill structure so that I could switch off between tabby and twill. I tried not to plan too far ahead, so the pattern was a bit scattered at first, but I decided to add some tapestry inlay and then fell into a rhythm.

I used a clasped weft technique to integrate those thicker brown pieces of yarn into the weft. Later, I decided to hand-weave pieces vertically as well to create the grid. I also added some gold embroidery to finish and add some more interest.

I'm pretty happy with the result, especially for not having planned ahead! Next step is to find a way to mount it to the wall.
